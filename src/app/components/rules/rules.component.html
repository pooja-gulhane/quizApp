Quiz Rules

  • There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
  • Once you submit the quiz, you cannot change your answers.
  • All questions are mandatory, and you cannot skip any questions.
  • After the quiz, you will be able to see your score and review the correct answers.
  • If you exit the quiz mid-way, your progress will be saved.
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection before starting the quiz.
  • All results are final once submitted, and no appeals will be entertained.
  • Ensure your browser's pop-up blocker is turned off for a smooth experience.
  • Ensure you are in a distraction-free environment while taking the quiz.
  • In case of technical difficulties, reach out to support immediately.
